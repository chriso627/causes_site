Sample Letter Asking for Tuition Assistance:
Who shall I contact? Online Tuition Assistance or Face to Face?
There's absolutely no point writing sample letters asking for tuition assistance if you don't have anyone to send them to! Before you start to write sample letters asking for tuition assistance take the time to do some research, create a spreadsheet and on the tabs clearly mark millionaires, churches, charities and government grants. Under each tab find 10 people or organisations to write to and then list their contact details. Keeping track of who you contact will really help you contact as many people as possible for money and maximise your chances of getting a lot of money. Strategise and have tactics in place before you write sample letters asking for tuition assistance.
